Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Focus3D (referred to as "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to the Crank mobile application.

2. Data Collection

Crank is designed as a professional utility tool for camera departments. We believe in absolute privacy:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • No Account Tracking: There are no user accounts, and we do not track your identity across other apps or websites.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use third-party tracking or analytics services that harvest your data.

3. Device Permissions

Crank may request access to certain device features to provide its core functionality (e.g., Camera access for exposure calculations or augmented reality features). These permissions are used exclusively within the app session on your device. No images or sensor data are ever uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

4. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data risk associated with using Crank.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
